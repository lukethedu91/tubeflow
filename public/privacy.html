Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Vid Planner ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and application.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Password (hashed and encrypted)
  • User profile name (optional)

Application Data

  • Video projects, ideas, and planning notes
  • Search history for YouTube videos
  • Generated images and content preferences

Usage Information

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referrer information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • To authenticate your account and prevent fraud
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To serve you relevant content and advertisements
  • To send you service-related notifications

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore with encryption in transit and at rest. We follow industry-standard security practices including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Firestore security rules limiting access to your own data
  • Regular security audits and updates

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication and data storage
  • Google Analytics: For usage analytics (anonymized)
  • Google AdSense: For serving advertisements
  • YouTube API: To search and display public videos

6. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data (right to be forgotten)
  • Opt-out: Opt out of promotional emails and tracking

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@vidplanner.com

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and analytics to understand how you use our service. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may limit functionality.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account data indefinitely unless you request deletion. Usage analytics are retained for 24 months then deleted.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time. We will notify users of significant changes via email.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at privacy@vidplanner.com
